Mynance

SwiftUI finance tracker for budgeting and expense tracking

Overview

Mynance is a SwiftUI finance tracker app that helps users manage their income and spending, featuring transaction tracking, CoreData storage, and a modern iOS UI.

Technologies

  • Swift & SwiftUI
  • CoreData for persistence
  • MVVM Architecture
  • Custom UI & Themes
