StudyMate

AI-driven productivity and budgeting app for students

Overview

StudyMate is an iOS productivity suite combining a study planner, budgeting tool, and AI helper in one cohesive platform. It stores data locally, tracks goals, and supports daily reminders.

Features

  • AI-powered chat for academic help
  • Budget tracker and course manager
  • CoreData storage & dynamic dark mode
